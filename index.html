Mexican taco

Mexican taco
3 tomatoes, 2 onions, 2 bell peppers, 1 pound ground beef,
2 tablespoons of cooking wine, 2 pinches of salt and pepper,
3 cloves garlic, 1 cup of tomatoe sauce and 2 pinches of parsley.

To make these delicious tacos, follow these steps:
1. Dice all the veggies.
2. Massage in the cooking wine, salt and pepper into the meat.
3. Turn on your oven to medium high.
4. Put in the garlic and diced onions, cook for 5 minutes.
5. Put in the meat, and mix them together, cook for 5 minutes.
6. Put in everything else. (not the sauce), cook for 20 minutes over medium heat.
7. Mix in the sauce and parsley.
*. Stuff them in some tortillas! Chao.

Roast Pork and Pickled Cucumber Sandwich

Roast Pork and Pickled Cucumber Sandwich
1 pound boneless pork loin, 1/2 teaspoon cayenne pepper, kosher
salt and black pepper, 1 cucumber, thinly sliced, 3 tablespoons cider,
vinegar, 2 tablespoons olive oil, 2 teaspoons brown sugar, 1/3 cup
mayonnaise, 4 Portuguese rolls, split

To make these fulfilling sandwiches, follow these steps:
1. Heat oven to 450* F. Season the pork with the cayenne and 1/4 teaspoon salt. Roast on a rimmed baking
sheet until an instant-read thermometer inserted in the center reaches 145* F, 20 to 25 minutes; thinly slice.
2. Meanwhile, combine the cucumber, vinegar, oil, brown sugar, and 1/4 teaspoon each salt and black pepper
in a medium bowl.
3. Divide the mayonnaise, pork, and cucumber among the rolls.

Smoked Salmon Salad Sandwich

Smoked Salmon Salad Sandwich
8 ounces skinless salmon fillet, 6 ounces smoked salmon, chopped,
1/3 cup mayonnaise, 1/3 cup chopped celery, 1 tablespoon chopped chives, 1
tablespoon chopped capers, 1 teaspoon grated lemon zest, black pepper,
lettuce leaves and 8 slices pumpernickel bread

To make these delicious sandwiches, follow these steps:
1. Heat oven to 450* F. Roast the salmon fillet on a rimmed baking sheet until cooked through, 6 to 8 minutes;
let cool, then flake.
2. Combine the smoked salmon, mayonnaise, celery, chives, capers, lemon zest, flaked salmon, and 1/4 teaspoon
pepper in a medium bowl.
3. Divide the salmon salad and lettuce leaves among the bread.

Tomato, Bacon, and Garlic Mayo Sandwich

Tomato, Bacon, and Garlic Mayo Sandwich
8 slices bacon 1/3 cup plain Greek yogurt, 1 tablespoon chopped
oregano, 1 small clove garlic, minced, kosher salt, 2 beefsteak, tomatoes, sliced,
1 avocado, sliced, lettuce leaves, 8 slices white sandwich bread, toasted

1. Cook the bacon in batches in a large skillet over medium heat until crisp, 12 to 15 minutes.
2. Combine the yogurt, oregano, garlic, and 1/4 teaspoon salt in a small bowl.
3. Divide the bacon, garlic mayonnaise, tomatoes, avocado, and lettuce leaves among the bread.

French Ham and Cheese Sandwich

French Ham and Cheese Sandwich
1/3 cup finely chopped cornichons, 4 tablespoons (1/2 stick)
unsalted butter, at room temperature, 1/4 cup Dijon mustard,
2 tablespoons chopped tarragon, black pepper, 8 ounces sliced
deli ham, 4 ounces Comte or Gruyere, sliced and 1 large baguette, split

1. Combine the cornichons, butter, mustard, tarragon, and 1/4 teaspoon pepper in a small bowl.
2. Spread the pickle mixture on the baguette. Top with the ham and cheese. Cut into 4 sandwiches.
